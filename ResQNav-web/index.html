How ResQNav Works
01

Detect & Report

Users report accidents via the AI Chatbot with geotagged images and live GPS. Our system verifies incidents using spatial database checks.

02

Verify & Alert

Custom filters eliminate false reports. Verified incidents instantly alert hospitals, fire stations, and nearby emergency services.

03

Reroute & Rescue

Emergency responders get priority green corridors. Commuters are dynamically rerouted to clear the path — saving lives and time.
